Career Fair

Held each quarter, the Career Fair connects students with leading companies from across the monster world. It’s the perfect opportunity to showcase your scaring skills, network with recruiters, and learn about future internships and job opportunities. Whether you're exploring your options or ready to take the next big step, this event helps monsters of all majors prepare for their professional journey.

Welcome Week

At the start of each semester, Welcome Week helps new students settle into campus life with a variety of fun, community-focused events. From dorm socials to campus scavenger hunts, it’s all about making connections and feeling at home. Returning students volunteer to guide newcomers, ensuring everyone begins their Monsters University journey with confidence and plenty of smiles.

Leadership Summit

The annual Leadership Summit brings together students from every department to discuss ideas that shape the university’s future. Workshops, panel discussions, and spirited debates help attendees sharpen their communication skills and confidence. It’s a forum for ambitious minds who want to make a lasting impact on the Monsters University community.

Fall Talent Showcase

The Fall Talent Showcase is a campus favorite, celebrating the creativity and hidden talents of the student body. From musical performances to comedy and dance, this event lets every monster shine under the spotlight. It’s a night of laughter, applause, and unforgettable acts that remind everyone why Monsters University is full of personality.

Innovation Expo

The Innovation Expo features student projects that blend creativity, science, and scare-tech. Teams present their inventions and research to faculty and peers, showcasing how academic curiosity drives real-world monster solutions. It’s a celebration of discovery, collaboration, and the bright (and slightly spooky) ideas that push the monster world forward.

Scare Games

The Scare Games are Monsters University’s ultimate test of skill, courage, and teamwork. This thrilling campus tradition brings together fraternities and sororities from across the university to compete in a series of high-stakes challenges designed to push every monster’s scaring abilities to the limit. From stealthy sneak tests to fearsome fright rounds, the Scare Games celebrate the determination, strategy, and school spirit that define the best scarers in training.
